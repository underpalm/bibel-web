About Rose

Rose exists to help people understand the Bible when reading feels hard, time is short, or there is no one nearby to ask.

We believe Bible study should be easier to begin without becoming shallow. Rose lets you ask a question by voice or text, then brings you back to Scripture with simple language, context, and verses you can check for yourself.

Scripture first

Rose is designed to point back to the biblical text, not replace it.

Plain language

Answers should be understandable for busy people, new readers, and non-readers.

Made for voice

The app starts with the simplest interaction: ask out loud and listen.
